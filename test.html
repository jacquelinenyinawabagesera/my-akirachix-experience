My First Week at AkiraChix

Onboarding Process

The onboarding process at AkiraChix was seamless and welcoming. From meeting our mentors to getting acquainted with the curriculum, everything was well-organized and supportive.

First Class: Computer Basics

We started with the basics of computer science, learning about the essential components and their functions. This laid a solid foundation for our future studies.

Linda's SDLC Talk

Linda's talk on the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) was eye-opening. She assigned us a research task, to be done in groups to deepen our understanding of the process, which has been incredibly informative. We particulary chose SafeBoda, a motorcycle hailing app.

Exploring Safeboda

My team and I reasearched about SafeBoda, understanding how technology is being used to improve transportation safety. It's amazing to see real-world applications of what we're learning.

Creating Emails, Docs, Slides, Calendar, and Meet

We dove into practical skills by creating emails, documents, slides, calendars, and using Meet for virtual collaboration. These tools are essential for our academic and professional journeys.

Highlights of the Week

  1. Friendly and supportive mentors
  2. Comprehensive onboarding process
  3. Hands-on computer basics class
  4. Insightful SDLC talk by Linda
  5. Practical sessions on productivity tools

Areas for Improvement

  • Limited seating in the classroom
  • Occasional technical issues during virtual sessions
  • Short breaks between sessions
